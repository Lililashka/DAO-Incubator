The Experiment

DAOincubator brings together builders, users, researchers and investors, channeling their zeal into experiments that yield real-world implementations.

We aim to provide individualized attention to our advisees and to maintain a field of critical research and practical knowledge that grows together with the rest of the space. We’re building a coordinated ecosystem of open collaboration, where people with a wide range of specializations and viewpoints can be tapped to help develop projects and ideas. By documenting our progress and our observations, we will also propagate our learning to the larger ecosystem in order to advance the thinking around the DAO design space.

Ecosystem

Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs) are a broad category of entities that live on blockchain networks. Well known DAO use cases include mining pools and decentralized exchanges, though there are lesser known DAOs perform functions ranging from maintaining stablecoin pegs to rewarding memers.

In the current landscape, DAO builders can collaborate around shared challenges, find inspiration in experiments and develop new methodologies for organizational innovation. In order to move towards a post-firm future, we must develop a post-entrepreneurial (anti)discipline. To design organizations of the future, we must help the designers completely rewrite the curriculum. DAOincubator is supporting this progress by advancing thinking around the commons, self-sovereign identity, fairness, and competitive autonomous processes.

We’re also working with several groups in the space to map out the decentralized governance landscape. Drop us a line if you want to participate!
